Privacy Policy

pwdr is a password manager that operates entirely within your browser. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to ensuring your data remains secure and private.

Local Data Storage

This extension stores your secret phrase and all configuration data locally within your browser's storage. No data is ever transmitted to any external server or third party.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, log, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information or any user data.

Permissions

All permissions requested by the extension are strictly necessary for its core functionality. We do not use permissions for tracking or data collection.
